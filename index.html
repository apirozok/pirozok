EncuentroAyuda.com & EstudiosBiblico.org

A Comprehensive Digital Outreach and Discipleship Strategy

Overview

From 2022 to 2024, I collaborated with Cary Palmon Ministries to develop EncuentroAyuda.com and EstudiosBiblico.org, two integrated platforms designed to expand the ministry’s global reach and nurture a growing community of spiritual seekers.

EncuentroAyuda.com: Meeting Immediate Needs

  • Felt-Needs Content: A curated library of resources to address common struggles with practical and spiritual solutions.
  • Free Ebooks: Visitors registered to access ebooks, offering biblical encouragement and guidance.

This platform acted as a gateway, connecting seekers by addressing their immediate needs and inviting them into the ministry's community.

EstudiosBiblico.org: Nurturing Spiritual Growth

Complementing EncuentroAyuda.com, EstudiosBiblico.org provided discipleship resources for ongoing spiritual growth through study materials and faith-strengthening tools.

Strategy and Technology Integration

Key Contributions:

  • Website Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, API integrations, and SEO optimization.
  • Google Ads Grant Management: Managed a $10,000/month budget, generating 458,882 clicks with an 8.16% CTR.
  • SEO Optimization: Delivered 1,794 organic clicks, 17,281 impressions, and a 10.4% CTR.
  • Email Marketing: Created workflows and segmentation strategies using Constant Contact.
  • Analytics: Tracked performance with Google Analytics and HubSpot Analytics.
  • Digital Missionary Assistant (DMA): Deployed AI to filter and prioritize responses for meaningful engagement.

Results and Impact

  • Added over 500,000 contacts to the ministry’s database, creating a vital resource for evangelism and discipleship.
  • Boosted website visibility and engagement through integrated strategies.
  • Created a seamless pathway for seekers to grow spiritually through practical outreach and discipleship.
